Portfolio > Digital Prototyping

This project was produced as an individual project as part of a Masters of Interaction Design at The University of Queensland.

Meow Meow Cat is a game I developed for a digital prototyping subject.

It is a single-player, side-scrolling game. Players take on the role of the cat, Meow Meow, and move her along by stepping on the corresponding coloured dot on a floor mat to reach the sunshine.

View this project on GitHub

Prototypes

Four prototypes were constructed for this project, a video and three interactive prototypes. ActionScript 3 and a Makey Makey device were used to produce a high-fidelity, fixed-path prototype for users can interact with.

Each prototype tested a specific element of the game, simulating or omitting parts that weren't related to what was being tested.

Testing the game involved a series of sessions with users. Each session had the same process, script, and survey to evaluate the game.
