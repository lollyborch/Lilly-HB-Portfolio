Portfolio Inner

Approach

As a graphic designer with many years of experience, I have produced many portfolios - electronic and printed. When approaching this portfolio, I asked the question: What is the difference between presenting graphic design work and interaction design work? The answer, I found, was telling the story and presenting the process, not just the end artefacts.

I started by assessing my current folio and looking at what worked and what didn’t.

>

Tell the story and the process,
don't just present the end artefacts.

What worked

  • Grid layout to easily see multiple projects
  • Snapshot of skills and experience
  • Typing text intro

What didn’t

  • Projects are dispalyed as lightboxes of final designs without context or process; How will I alter the grid layout of projects to include process information?

Undecided

  • Single page site with sticky navigation linked to anchors on the page (research to why this is bad)
  • Bootstrap framework. I love Bootstrap but am unable to use it in this assignment so need to figure out an alternate way to create grid layout.
