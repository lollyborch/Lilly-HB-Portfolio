Smart Track

masters project

Portfolio > Smart Track

This project was produced in a small team as part of a Masters of Interaction Design at The University of Queensland.

Working collaboratively within a small team, I helped design and prototype a physical, interactive and experiential concept for the new terminal at the Brisbane Airport. We were tasked with devising an interactive product or system that delivers an innovative, public and social user experience.

Methods employed:

  • Direct user observation & tracking
  • Interviews
  • Personas
  • Scenarios & storyboards
  • Concept Mapping
  • Body Storming
  • Design Sprints
  • Prototyping
  • User Workshops

The Smart Track app

After extensive research, concept ideation, and user feedback, our team narrowed down the problem space to baggage collection, as this ranks as a top “bug” for many passengers. We addressed this problem with Smart Track, a luggage status tracking and notification app.

The Smart Track app allows users to login with thier booking details or by scanning their boarding pass. All passengers on the booking are displayed along with how many peices of luggage they have checked. Users can also toggle notifications on/off to alert them when their bag reaches checkpoints throughout the process. To help coordinate passenger pick-up, friends and family can also use the app to receive alerts on when passengers have collected their bags and are ready to be collected.
